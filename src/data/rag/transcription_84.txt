foreign we will be talking about Champion Summoners Summoners are spells that you can take to further enhance your Champion's abilities and role they can be used to increase your utility Mobility survivability or damage before we jump into the specific abilities let's talk about ways to reduce some of your cooldowns one way you can reduce the cooldown time of Summoners is through the summoner's Insight Mastery this works on all Summoners and gives up to a 10 cooldown reduction another way to reduce certain Summoners cooldowns is through the Distortion boot enchantment this will reduce the cooldown of flash ghost and teleport by 20 and also give them additional effects flash is by far the most useful Mobility Summoner in the game it allows you to quickly teleport your Champion to a Target location within range you can use it to instantly Dodge abilities close gaps quickly or teleport over obstacles and Terrain flash can be used both defensively and offensively and has a cooldown of 5 minutes and 400 range you can pretty much take flash on any champion flash is best used in shorter trays as opposed to Extended trades or trades involving a large amount of kiting ghost allows you to move 27 faster and avoid unit Collision for 10 seconds it has a 210 second cooldown and is self-cast is useful for extended trades chases and escapes while it lasts longer than flash it only increases your movement speed and doesn't allow you to teleport over Terrain ghost is generally taken on specific Champions to benefit that Champion's strengths an example would be senister Vlad who kite well and benefit more from the speed rather than a short instant teleport and extended trades I suggest taking ghosts on Champions where kiting or chasing is a large part of their kit a few examples are singed uder Vladimir or hecarim teleport allows you to teleport your Champion to a non-champion friendly Target after a 4 second Channel this includes targets like friendly Towers minions Wards and more you can also teleport to certain targets like Jarvan standard teleport has a five minute cooldown and has a global range and can be cast anywhere on the map assuming you have a friendly non-champion Target to teleport to you can cancel teleport by activating the spell again but this will put it on a 200 second cooldown you should be taking teleport when you want to apply Global pressure to the map as you sacrifice a lot of your lane pressure taking teleport here are some important things to know about teleport teleporting to a friendly Tower successfully will give you a 240 second cooldown instead of the full 300 second cooldown teleporting to anything besides a tower will result in a full 300 second cooldown if a tower is destroyed as you are channeling your teleport will be canceled and put back on full cooldown if you are stunned rooted silenced feared or taunted during your teleport Channel your teleport will be canceled and put on cooldown when teleporting to a ward or friendly minion that Ward or friendly minion will become invulnerable for the full Channel combining all the Summoners into one video turned out to be a
