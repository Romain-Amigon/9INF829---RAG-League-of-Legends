Title: NEW DORAN'S ITEMS & BOOTS - League of Legends
URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OxPyvkadyjU

Content:
We got new boots, two new Doran's starting items, and two new runes returning with the start of season 2. First up, two new Doran's starting items. Doran's Bow gives AD attack speed and omnivamp. Doran's Helm gives health, armor, magic resist, and increases the damage of attacks to minions by five, just like Doran's Ring and Tear. Next up, new boots. Gluttonous Greaves give omnivamp and a passive that when you slay an enemy champion, you gain one extra omnivamp, stacking up to six times. And for mid laners, once they get upgraded, they get the now and forever passive, giving you 5% extra damage while above half health, and 15% increased healing, shielding, and regeneration while below. Quite interesting. Also, the mid quest reward got changed. Dusk and Dawn got changed, too, and Trailblazer and Opportunity got removed. Then Deathfire Touch and Stormraider's Surge are back as runes. Deathfire Touch makes your abilities go burn on enemy champions that increases after 3 seconds, and Stormraider replaces Phase Rush, giving a burst of move speed and slow resist when damaging 25% of an enemy champion's total health within 3 seconds. Also, you'll be able to select binds per champion or global soon, and the W A S D movement is also coming. That's about it for this season 2 start. Not too many changes, and the map seems to be back to the regular Rift, unfortunately. So, we are not getting a demon team Rift map skin.
