Here are five helpful tips that you might not know about Sona. Number one, auto attack reset. You can get a faster auto attack off if you have your power cord. You can auto attack, press Q, and then auto attack again instantly. This is insanely helpful in small fights and when you want to destroy a ward quicker. Number two, stacking her passive. Did you know you can actually get two stacks when you cast a W? The heal is one stack and the second stack is from the shield. Your ally has to take damage to the shields you provide them. And it has to be meaningful damage. It can't just be one or two damage. Which leads us into number three, insane ultimate cooldown. When your passive is fully stacked, every stack you obtain that would count towards your passive now reduces your ultimate cool down by 1.5 seconds. You can maximize this effects by shielding multiple of your allies on an objective like Baron Nasher. You're likely to get five or six stacks instantly. As Baron does enough damage to the shields to count as a stack, so your ultimate is instantly reduced by 10 or 12 seconds. Every cast of your Q can also gain two stacks if it hits two enemies, reducing your cool down by a further 3 seconds. Number four, Q aura. Aside from sending out her damaging bolts to enemies, Sona also makes her allies deal bonus magic damage on their next basic attack within the next 5 seconds. A lot of players don't know this. And number five, aura effects. If you have your summon area available, it will actually attach to your ally when they are affected by your Q or E aura. Aries shields your ally which will also proc your echo of helilia, dream maker, counterwards, dynams effects, and also spreads auras like ardent sensor and staff of flowing water.
