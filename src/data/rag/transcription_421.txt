Title: 3 Minute Janna Guide - A Guide for League of Legends
URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=R9snajqYYYg

Content:
Why play Janna support? Well, she brings great lockdown. She's amazing at keeping her carry alive. Her disengage is top tier, and she offers so much value in team fights. However, she isn't the strongest laning support, and her engage can be unreliable. Janna's first passive is she grants bonus movement speed to alli champions moving towards her. And her second passive is her attacks on hit and her W deals bonus magic damage, scaling with her bonus movement speed. Janna's Q places down a tornado at her feet, which can charge over 3 seconds. While charging, it increases its range, speed, damage, and knockup duration. After 3 seconds, or when reactivated, it becomes a straight line skill shot, which deals damage to enemies hit and knocks them up. Janna's W has a passive where she's always ghosted and gains bonus move speed. The active is a point-and-click ability, which deals damage to the target hit and slows them. Janna's E has a passive where if she slows or knocks up at least one enemy champion, 20% of this cool down is refunded. The active is a point-and-click ability which applies a shield to a turret, herself, or an ally. And while the shield holds, the target gains bonus attack damage. Janna's ultimate causes her to channel on the spot, knocking away all nearby enemies. She channels a large circle around herself for 3 seconds, healing herself and all allies within. For combos, your go-to is to attack into W and Q. Attack again, then alt to throw them into danger. And if starting Q, use Q, then attack into W, and attack into your alt. Of course, with all combos, using E on yourself or an ally as needed. For matchups, Janna is great into engage supports, easily interrupting their engages and punishing them, and she can struggle into some of the harder lane bullies. For runes, take this. Ary makes everything you do better. For build order, start World Atlas and Pot into a Moonstone Renewer, tier 2 boots, and upgrade to a dream maker. From here, Redemption and Battle Song are great pickups before finishing a build with any of these as needed. For skill order, start W then Q, then E before maxing W, then E, then Q, taking alt whenever you can. For summoner spells, take flash and heal. For synergies, she is amazing with hyper carries, easily peeling for them and keeping them alive while they win the fight for you. Starting the game as support, your job is to make life easy for your carry and difficult for the enemy. If you're against a hard engaged champion, this is perfect. You want to stay near your carry, ready to interrupt their engage and punish them if they go for it. If they ever go in, the amount of damage you can return after you slow them with W is brilliant. If you're against another enchanter, look to poke with W as often as possible and look for opportunities where you can land a Q and then go in for the fight. And if you're against a bully champion, focus on dodging their skill shot and shielding yourself and your carry. Early boots here are absolutely your friend. Janna is also a great Roma, so boots early regardless are very strong. Between your massive slow and knockup early, you're great at roaming with your jungler or even just ganking mid. Enter the mid game, take your tower and rotate mid. This puts you and your carry in the center of the map while you're much stronger and allows you to run around the map making plays. But what Janna is even better at is running around the map, interrupting her opponent's plays with easy disengage. Entering late game team fights, play as the backline support. Stay near your carry so you can constantly shield them as well as slowing, knocking up, and knocking away anyone coming for them.
